EDM Rights Selection Tool Recommendation

Based on your answers in the EDM Selection Tool it is recommended that you apply one of the following two Creative Commons licenses to the digital object:

Apply a Creative Commons Attribution licence to allow third parties to freely use the digital object for any purpose as long as they give you attribution by inserting the following rights value:

http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/

Apply a Creative Commons Attribution ShareAlike licence to require that third parties give you attribution and that modified works (remixes) are licensed under the same licence by inserting the following rights value:

http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/

Note: in both cases make sure that the selected licence terms are clearly communicated on your own website. You can find instructions on how to do this at the Creative Commons website.

You can also apply the Creative Commons Public Domain Dedication to your work. If you apply the CC0 Public Domain Dedication you irrevocably waive all your rights to the work, use this tool with caution.

Apply the Creative Commons Zero Public Domain Dedication to waive all rights to the work worldwide under copyright law, including all related and neighboring rights, to the extent allowed by law by inserting the following rights value:

https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

Please note that the Data Exchange Agreement requirement of CC0 only applies to metadata and to the digital objects. For more information please click here.
