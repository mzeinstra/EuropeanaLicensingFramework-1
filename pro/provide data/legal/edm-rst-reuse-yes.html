EDM Rights Selection Tool

The Europeana Data Model (EDM):rights Selection Tool helps Europeana data provider selecting one of the accepted rights statementsthat are used to indicate the rights status of objects in Europeana. Answer the questions (max. 6 questions) below to determine the EDM:rights value that applies to a single object.

Do you want to allow, or have permission to allow, third parties (end-users) to freely re-use the digital object for commercial and non-commercial purposes as well as to create derivative works?

Yes No

Note: Select 'yes' if you want to enable use of the digital object on Wikipedia.

Note: Apply the CC0 Public Domain Dedication if you want to completely waive your rights in the digital object and donate it to the public domain.
