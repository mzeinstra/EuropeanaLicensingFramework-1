EDM Rights Selection Tool Recommendation

Based on your answers in the EDM Selection Tool it is recommended that you apply one of the following two Creative Commons licences to the digital object:

In both cases the object may only be used for non-commercial purposes and the user will need to give you attribution.

Apply a Creative Commons Attribution NonCommercial licence to allow third parties to make available modified works (remixes) under their own conditions by inserting the following rights value:

http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc/3.0/

Apply a Creative Commons Attribution NonCommercial ShareAlike licence to require that modified works (remixes) are licensed under the same license as the digital object by inserting the following rights value:

http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/3.0/

Note: in both cases make sure that the selected licence terms are clearly communicated on your own website. You can find instructions on how to do this at the Creative Commons website.
