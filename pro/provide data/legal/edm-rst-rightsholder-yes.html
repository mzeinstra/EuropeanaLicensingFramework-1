EDM Rights Selection Tool

The Europeana Data Model (EDM):rights Selection Tool helps Europeana data provider selecting one of the accepted rights statements that are used to indicate the rights status of objects in Europeana. Answer the questions (max. 6 questions) below to determine the EDM:rights value that applies to a single object.

Do you want to allow, or have permission to allow, third parties (end-users) to re-use the digital object under certain conditions?
