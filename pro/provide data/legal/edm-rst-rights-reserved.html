EDM Rights Selection Tool Recommendation

Based on your answers in the EDM Selection Tool it is recommended that you apply one of the following three rights statements:

Apply the Rights Reserved - Free Access statement when the digital object is freely accessible on your website you should by inserting the following rights value:

http://www.europeana.eu/rights/rr-f.html

Apply the Rights Reserved - Paid Access statement when access to the digital object on your website requires a payment you should by inserting the following rights value:

http://www.europeana.eu/rights/rr-p.html

Apply the Rights Reserved - Restricted Access statement when access to the digital object on your website requires a login or is otherwise restricted you should by inserting the following rights value:

http://www.europeana.eu/rights/rr-r.html
